WORD RUNNER

[Itch.io Playable Build](https://waterkat.itch.io/word-runner)

This is an infinite runner where you jump over sharks, bugs, and chairs, and keep your head low below anchors.

You can jump, or dive (fast fall) to keep increasing your personal high score.

Type the words below the jump or dive command in order to jump/dive, and press enter in order to send the command.

This game was created with AI generated Imagery
